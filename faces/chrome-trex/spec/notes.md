# ChromeTRex

Dive-watch inspired prototype based on the supplied reference image.

## Intent

Capture the main visual language of the reference:
- monochrome steel bezel
- large lume blocks
- triangle marker at 1 o’clock area on bezel and 2 o’clock area on dial
- snowflake-style hands
- compact text stack in the upper-right quadrant
- small Chrome dino motif above 6 o’clock

## Notes

This is a browser prototype, not a 1:1 branded reproduction.
The visual structure is the target; brand text and logo treatment are adapted for the repo.
